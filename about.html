I’m Felicia, an UX designer, urban planner and creative thinker.

About me

I’ve always had a passion for technology and design, especially when the two can be combined. I’m fascinated with the way color, forms and space can be expressed in different ways to have an impact and to solve problems. This curiosity has led me to become a designer and an engineer. I'm constantly seeking new experiences to influence and inspire me. I am intrigued by how aesthetics and style change with technology and culture, and how you can take principles of the past and apply them to create something new and original.

Design combines my joy of problem solving with my love of visual aesthetics, both in the physical realm as in digital media. Design should not only look great but also have a purpose that attracts and engage users, one that connects and supports the overall purpose of the design. Over the past years, I’ve used these principles to create digital and print materials to help brands shape their visual identity. I'm also working as an urban planner where I’m using these principles to develop and create places for people from a human-centered design perspective.

As a final note, I'm curious in my search for knowledge, always seeking to be inspired.
