I’m Felicia, an UX designer, urban planner and creative thinker.

About me

I’m a creative engineer with skills and competencies within urban planning and digital design. I’m fascinated with the way color, forms and space can be expressed in different ways to have an impact and to solve problems. I’m intrigued by how aesthetics and style change with technology and culture, and how you can take principles of the past and apply them to create something new and original.

Design combines my joy of problem solving with my love of visual aesthetics, both in the physical realm as in digital media. Design should not only look great but also have a purpose that attracts and engage users, one that connects and supports the overall purpose of the design. Over the past years, I’ve used these principles to create digital and print materials. I’m also working as an urban planner where I’m create places for people from a human-centered design perspective.

I’m an idealist who always tries to see something positive and looking for ways to improve the condition of things. I understand and share my ideas through images and illustrations. I'm good at visualizing, simplifying and making things clear, a strength that is well suited to creative and complex challenges. As a final note, I'm curious in my search for knowledge, always seeking to be inspired.
