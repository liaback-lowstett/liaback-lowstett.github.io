Book a meeting room

Book a meeting room is a progressive web app that was developed during a course between january and may 2019 together with The Swedish School of Library and Information Science at Borås University.

My role

I was part of a team where we together were responsible for the experience strategy and design of the prototype. I lead the UX work and developed a Hi-fi prototype in Sketch.

Challenge

The library is an essential place where students can meet up and study together. However, the amount of space is limited, and working places and meeting rooms are well sought after. Today's digital booking system is limited, covering the need to book a room, but isn't flexible and doesn't enable the user to find a room based on current needs or individual preferences. The challenged concerned the issue of how to create a modern library by making meeting rooms effective, dynamic, and flexible to use.

Approache

To come up with and to develop the idea of an improved booking system we have applied a Human-centered design perspective, working through the phases of inspiration and ideation.

Initial, during our Inspiration phase we watched and listened to our target group through interviews and observations in order to better understand the users and to get smart on our challenge.

During the Ideation phase, we started to make sense of everything we had observed and went into generate ideas through methods such as team idea mapping. To identify opportunities for design and to test and refine our solution we created personas to guide design decisions, priorities and create empathy amongst our focus group. From our personas, we created user requirements which makes up the cornerstones of our app.

The prototype was developed through an iterative process, going from a low-fi prototype to being developed as a hi-fi prototype. The low-fi prototype concerned the structure of the app, like hierarchy, navigation and user flows. The hi-fi prototype concerned user interface and feel, including layout, font, color, pictures etc.

Solution

Through the app, users can book, find, share, confirm, and cancel meeting rooms in an easy and accessible way. When booking a meeting room the user can choose a room based on several features like size, equipment and location. The app makes the booking system more effective and dynamic by adding features which makes it easier to confirm, cancel and share meetings and meeting rooms. If a meeting gets canceled there is a function which lets other students know when room gets available with short notice. By adding social features the user can send an invitation to others who will attend the meeting. The user can also add the meeting directly to its calendar, which make it compatible to other apps on the device.
