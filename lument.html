Lument

The Lumentin® concept renders perfect negative contrast in CT-examinations of the small intestine formerly only possible with MR imaging. Examinations can be made quicker, cheaper, and with a minimum of discomfort for the patients. Lumentin® is also a strong candidate as a universal contrast agent for gastrointestinal imaging. Sales in the European Union are planned to start in 2023.

My role

My role was to create a digital visual identity and to design and develop a website using Figma, TypeScript, HTML and CSS.

Approache

The design is based on an iterative process including meetings and workshops with the client to create a shared mental image of how to develop the website. The design was developed by creating mockups, wireframes and a hi-fi prototype.

Solution

The website was developed according to conventional user interface design guidelines. It's responsive on different devices and easy to navigate. The interface have a cohesive graphic profile.
