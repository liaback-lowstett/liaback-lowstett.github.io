EdgeEx Vol. 5

EdgeEx Vol. 5 is a festive event with inspiring talks for employes at Netlighters, clients, students and partners with a total of 300 people. The theme was all about the magic of a circus and the event took place in a circus tent.

My role

My role was to create a poster with the event moto and information about where the event would take place and at what time and date. Except from that, I was asked to set free all my creativity.

Approache

I started to research previous events and its design and how Netlight usually are branding themselves. I took inspiration from the circus realm and everything it includes, from horses to popcorn and all the people that creates the show or that comes and visit it. After the inspirational research I experimented with colors and font until a got the look I was aiming for. Last but not least i started to put everything together in indesign and illustrator.

Solution

The poster consist of a background inspired by the pattern of a circus tent that draws the attention towards the information in the middle of the poster. The information text is framed by a playful and nostalgic frame which together with the font enhance the circus theme. To balance the layout, two acrobats are framing the poster, swinging from the roof. The color scheme is based on a blue-green and red color palette which adds to the playfulness. It's all about the circus!
