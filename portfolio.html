My process

Born and raised in Stockholm, Sweden, I’ve always had a passion for technology and design. Ever since I can recall, I have been fascinated with the way color, type and space relate to information or how it can impact a mood. This curiosity has led me to become an ambitious designer and engineer who is always evolving. I am constantly seeking new experiences to influence and inspire me. I am intrigued by how aesthetics and style change with technology and culture or how people take principles of the past and apply them to create something unique and modern.

Design combines my joy of problem solving with my love of visual aesthetics. Design should not only look great but also have a purpose that attracts viewers with an engaging personality, one that connects and supports the overall purpose of the design. Over the past years, I’ve used these principles to create digital and print materials to help brands shape their visual identity across multiple platforms.

As a final note, I am relentless in my search for knowledge, I am always seeking to be challenged and inspired. Be curious!
