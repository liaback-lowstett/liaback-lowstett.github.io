It's Re:Leased

It's Re:Leased is a fashion rental subscription offering access to premium scandi-fashion. Renting is the best way to discover new brands and styles and get that variation you crave, without treating clothes as disposable. We give you access to great clothes at a price both you and mother earth can afford, on a plan that fits your lifestyle. Less climate anxiety, more self expression.

My role

I drive the end-to-end design process and develop new features in Next.js and Tailwind CSS. I have developed the product through research and usertesting, create mockups, wireframes, and prototypes of new interfaces.

Approach

The design is based on an iterative process including workshops with the team to create a shared mental image of how to develop the product. The design was developed by research, user flows, wireframes, and a hi-fi prototype to do user tests on.
